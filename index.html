About Chapman University

Chapman University is dedicated to empowering students with knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in a dynamic world. Join our vibrant community today!

Our Mission

Chapman University is dedicated to creating a dynamic learning environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, ethical responsibility, and global citizenship. We empower our students with the skills and knowledge to lead with integrity and impact their communities positively.

Our History

Founded in 1861, Chapman University has been a beacon of academic excellence for over 150 years. From its humble beginnings to becoming a nationally recognized institution, Chapman continues to uphold its tradition of innovation and commitment to student success.

Why Choose Chapman?

  • Vibrant campus life and a supportive community
  • State-of-the-art facilities and a picturesque campus
